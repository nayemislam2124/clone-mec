Muslim Entrepreneurs Community

To empower muslim entrepreneurs

Our mission is to foster innovation, growth, and collaboration among Muslim entrepreneurs & professionals

Objectives

Provide educational content and resources that help members learn new skills and stay up-to-date on industry trends.

Facilitate networking opportunities for members to connect with other entrepreneurs, mentors, investors, and business experts.

Create a platform for members to showcase their businesses, products, and services and receive feedback and support from the community.

Offer access to funding, mentorship, and other resources that help members grow their businesses.

Foster a culture of collaboration and knowledge-sharing that encourages members to support and learn from each other.

Mission

To create a thriving and supportive community of muslim entrepreneurs that fosters innovation, growth, and collaboration.

Vision

To be the go-to resource for Muslim entrepreneurs or potential Entrepreneurs seeking knowledge, connections, and inspiration to build successful businesses.

Goal

To empower Muslim entrepreneurs in Bangladesh through networking, education, and access to resources that help them navigate the challenges of building and growing a business.

Accomplished Event

02 September IT Career Seminar

The purpose of this seminar is to provide graduate or graduating students or even people from other disciplines with the information and resources they need to pursue a career in IT.
